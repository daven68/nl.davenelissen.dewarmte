Monitor your DeWarmte heat pump with Homey.

This unofficial app connects to your existing DeWarmte account and adds your heat pump as a read-only Homey device. It shows the current supply temperature, target supply temperature, operating status and general fault status.

Use Flow to respond when the heat pump turns on or off, when the target supply temperature changes, or to check the current status and target temperature.

Features

- Read-only heat pump status: on or off
- Current supply temperature
- Target supply temperature
- General fault indication
- Flow triggers and conditions for status and temperature

This first version is read-only and does not control or change your heat pump settings. The app will be expanded step by step with more DeWarmte insights, Flow options and additional functionality.

Compatibility: this version has been tested with a DeWarmte pompAO. Other DeWarmte heat pump models have not yet been tested.
